Our Vision

To Tatou Tirohanga

Westlake Boys Highschool in its Locality

To create a rich, collaborative culture that unites kura (schools), pouako (teachers), ākonga (students) and whānau to work collectively on common goals for the benefit of all

'Ehara taku toa i te toa takitahi, engari he toa takitini' - success is not in the work of an individual, but the work of many.

'RANGITOTO'
nā Johnny Waititi
Rangitoto te maunga Waitematā te moana Ngāti Paoa te iwi e Ko Pupuke te roto Anei ngā kura o Te Kāhui Ako E ngā iwi, tēnā koutou (2x)
English Translation:
Rangitoto is the mountain Waitemata is the ocean Ngati Paoa are the iwi Pupuke is the lake Here are the schools of our Community of learning Everyone welcome. Everyone, welcome.
